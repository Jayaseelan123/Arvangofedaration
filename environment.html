Environment

Protecting our natural heritage for a sustainable and greener future.

Problem Statement

Rapid soil degradation and increasing water scarcity are severely impacting local ecosystems and the livelihoods of rural communities. The lack of sustainable waste management further exacerbates environmental challenges in these areas.

What We Do

We promote sustainable conservation practices and facilitate the adoption of renewable energy at the village level. Our initiatives focus on rehabilitating land, conserving water, and creating a culture of environmental responsibility.

  • Reforestation and biodiversity protection.
  • Implementation of water conservation systems.
  • Rural waste management and recycling training.

Key Activities

Afforestation project

Community-led tree planting drives to restore local forests, increase green cover, and prevent soil erosion.

Rainwater Harvesting

Installing structural harvesting systems in schools and community centers to secure water during dry seasons.

Waste Management

Training rural households on waste segregation, composting, and reducing the use of single-use plastics.
