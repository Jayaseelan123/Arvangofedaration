Agriculture

Empowering farmers with sustainable methods and future-ready techniques.

Problem Statement

Small-scale farmers in rural areas struggle with low crop yields due to dependence on traditional, inefficient farming methods. Rising costs of inputs and climate unpredictability further threaten their livelihoods and food security.

What We Do

We train farmers in organic farming and climate-resilient agricultural techniques to improve profitability and sustainability. Our approach focuses on low-cost, high-yield solutions that protect the soil and the farmer's future.

  • Promotion of chemical-free organic farming.
  • Establishing local seed banks for indigenous varieties.
  • Modernizing irrigation and soil Mental health management.

Key Activities

Organic Certification

Assisting small farmers in obtaining organic certifications to access premium markets and increase income.

Indigenous Seed Banks

Creating community-managed seed banks to preserve local crop varieties that are resilient to regional climate conditions.

Technical Workshops

On-field training sessions on drip irrigation, vermicomposting, and natural pest management strategies.

Farmer Training & Empowerment

Providing training to small and marginal farmers, including Scheduled Tribe communities and farmers living in hilly regions, on:

Organic farming

Sustainable and chemical-free agricultural practices for healthier soil and yields.

Value Addition
