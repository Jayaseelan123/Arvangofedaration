About Us

Empowering NGOs, Building a Stronger Future

About the Federation

Collaborating for structural excellence in the social sector.

The ARVA NGO Federation was established to address the critical gaps in organizational capacity, transparency, and resource access for grassroots NGOs in India. We believe that small, rural organizations are the real drivers of change, but they often lack the systemic support needed to thrive in a complex regulatory environment.

Our federation serves as a unified platform where NGOs can seek guidance on legal compliance, professional proposal writing, and sustainable fundraising strategies. By fostering a culture of collaboration over competition, we leverage collective intelligence to solve systemic problems in education, Mental health, and rural development.

Vision & Mission

Defining our purpose and our path forward.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable and empowered society through strong NGOs ensuring quality education, Mental health, environmental protection, and inclusive growth for women and youth.

Our Mission

To strengthen NGOs and promote education, Mental healthcare, sustainable agriculture, environmental conservation, and empowerment of women and youth through collaboration, capacity building, and community-driven initiatives.

Our Objectives

Strategic goals that drive our daily operations.

Capacity Building

Legal & Compliance

Representing the collective interests of grassroots NGOs at policy levels.
