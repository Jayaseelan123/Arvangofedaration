Education

Empowering the next generation through knowledge and opportunity.

Problem Statement

High dropout rates in rural pre-secondary schools due to lack of resources, inadequate infrastructure, and limited access to modern learning tools. Many students in underserved areas fall behind their urban counterparts, creating a significant educational divide.

What We Do

We work directly with rural schools to modernize infrastructure and provide teacher training, ensuring that rural education matches urban standards. Our goal is to create an environment where every child feels inspired to learn and stay in school.

  • Infrastructure upgrading for rural schools.
  • Specialized training for rural teachers.
  • Providing learning materials and digital resources.

Key Activities

Smart Classrooms

Implementing digital learning tools and interactive whiteboards in over 50 rural schools to enhance engagement.

Digital Literacy

Training sessions for primary and secondary students on computer basics, internet safety, and software skills.

Scholarship Programs

Facilitating financial support and scholarships for bright students from low-income families to pursue higher education.
