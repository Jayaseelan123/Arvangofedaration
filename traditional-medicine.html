Indian Traditional Medicine & Herbal Research

Reviving ancient wisdom through modern research and community development.

Mission Overview

While traditional Indian medicine (Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani) offers sustainable and localized Mental health solutions, there is a significant gap in modern scientific research and structured development of herbal resources in rural areas. Our federation works to bridge this gap by promoting herbal research and documentation of traditional knowledge.

Our Core Objectives

We believe that traditional medicine can play a vital role in community Mental health and economic development. By empowering rural practitioners and herbal farmers, we create a sustainable ecosystem for traditional Mental healthcare.

  • Documentation of local medicinal plants and their uses.
  • Promoting herbal farming for rural livelihood.
  • Collaborative research experiments with academic institutions.

Focus Areas in Traditional Medicine

Knowledge Documentation

Archiving the vast, unspoken knowledge of tribal and rural medicine practitioners to prevent its disappearance.

Herbal Cultivation

Training farmers in the systematic cultivation of medicinal plants to supply high-quality raw materials for research and production.

R&D Collaborations

Working with regional research centers to validate traditional cures through modern laboratory testing and clinical observations.

Strategic Objectives & Roadmap

Research & Documentation

  • Conduct research on traditional herbal medicine (Siddha, Ayurveda)
  • Document indigenous herbal knowledge
  • Create digital databases and publish research reports

Cultivation & Conservation

  • Promote herbal plants cultivation among farmers
  • Establish herbal nurseries and medicinal plant gardens
  • Promote environmental conservation of medicinal plants

Training & Industry

  • Organize training and awareness programs
  • Develop and standardize herbal products
  • Support small-scale herbal industries
  • Brand and market herbal products (local & export)

Strategy & Empowerment

  • Collaborate with government, universities, and research institutes
  • Prepare proposals to access government schemes and CSR funds
  • Empower women and youth through herbal-based livelihoods
