Our Vision-Driven Approach

We work at the intersection of NGOs, foundations, and government to build a more transparent and effective social sector in India.

Who We Are

ARVA NGO Federation is more than an organization; it's a movement to professionalize the non-profit sector. By providing NGOs with the tools they need to stay compliant, fundraise effectively, and manage project transparently, we ensure that every rupee spent makes a real difference.

Our community-driven approach ensures that even the smallest rural NGOs have access to the same level of support as large international organizations.
